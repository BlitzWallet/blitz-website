Effortlessly Pay Friends.

Paying friends with Bitcoin should be simple. With Blitz Wallet, there’s no need to deal with invoices, QR codes, or copying long addresses—we handle the details so you can focus on what matters: sending Bitcoin seamlessly.

Easily Spend Bitcoin.

One of the biggest challenges with Bitcoin is finding places to spend it. That’s why Blitz Wallet includes a built-in store where you can use Bitcoin directly. Purchase gift cards—including Visa gift cards—subscribe to premium AI tools without sharing personal information, or buy a VPN for private browsing. With Blitz Wallet, your Bitcoin isn’t just for holding—it’s for using.

Onboard Any Business.

More businesses should accept Bitcoin, and Blitz Wallet makes it easy. Every user gets a built-in point-of-sale system, allowing any business to start accepting Bitcoin in just a few taps. Whether you’re a store owner or helping your favorite shop, onboarding Bitcoin payments has never been easier.

Self-custodial.

Your Bitcoin belongs to you—no banks, no middlemen, no third-party control. Blitz Wallet is fully self-custodial, meaning only you have access to your funds. Whether you’re saving or spending, your money stays in your hands.

Customizable Payment Experince.

Bitcoin isn't one-size-fits-all, and Blitz Wallet gives you options. Easily send and receive Bitcoin using eCash, Liquid, or Lightning—all in one app. Need to switch networks? Internal transfers make it effortless.

Built for simplicity.

Bitcoin can seem complex, but it doesn’t have to be. Blitz Wallet is designed for everyone—whether you’re new to Bitcoin or an experienced user. No complicated setups, just an easy way to send, spend, and manage your Bitcoin.
