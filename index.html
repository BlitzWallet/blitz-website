Your Questions.

Answered.

What is Blitz Wallet?

Blitz Wallet is a self-custodial Bitcoin-only wallet, meaning only you control your funds. It combines multiple technologies to offer a seamless payment experience—letting you send Bitcoin to friends, spend it on gift cards and services, and even accept payments with a built-in point-of-sale system.

How is Blitz Wallet different?

Blitz Wallet is built for simplicity. Unlike other self-custodial wallets, it features a Contacts section for easy transactions with friends and a built-in shop to spend Bitcoin on gift cards and services—all without extra apps. Plus, Blitz manages Lightning channel liquidity for you and integrates eCash, Liquid, and Lightning to minimize each network’s trade-offs, ensuring the best Bitcoin payment experience.

What payment types does Blitz Wallet offer?

Currently Blitz Wallet supports receiving Bitcoin, Lightning, and Liquid payments and sending Bitcoin, Lightning, and Liquid payments.

Why is my lightning channel not opening?

To avoid opening a Lightning channel when on-chain fees are too high, Blitz Wallet includes a ‘Max Channel Open Fee’ setting. If you have enough balance but a channel hasn’t opened, you may need to increase this limit. You can find the setting under Settings (⚙️) > Bank > Settings.

Can I send or receive eCash?

No, Blitz Wallet offers eCash as a way to interact with the Lightning Network. In the future, support for sending and receiving eCash may be added.
