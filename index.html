Your Questions.

Answered.

What is Blitz Wallet?

Blitz Wallet is a self-custodial Bitcoin-only wallet, meaning only you control your funds. We use the Spark layer two network to offer a seamless payment experience—letting you send Bitcoin to friends, spend it on gift cards and services, and even accept payments with a built-in point-of-sale system.

How is Blitz Wallet different?

Blitz Wallet is built for simplicity. Unlike other self-custodial wallets, it features a Contacts section for easy transactions with friends and a built-in shop to spend Bitcoin on gift cards and services—all without extra apps.

What payment types does Blitz Wallet offer?

Currently Blitz Wallet supports receiving Bitcoin, Lightning, Spark and Liquid payments and sending Bitcoin, Lightning, and Spark payments.

What is an LNURL address and how do I find it?

An LNURL address lets other wallets send you Bitcoin easily. It looks like this: username@blitzwalletapp.com. To find it, go to the Receive page and tap the address below your QR code. You can also update your username and profile image there. Your LNURL name is the same as your Blitz contact name.
