Welcome back to our 14 Days of Blitz series, where each day we showcase a unique feature that makes Blitz Wallet more than just a Bitcoin wallet. Today’s spotlight is all about privacy — and how Blitz Wallet puts control back in your hands with Anonymous SMS messaing.

The Problem

When you sign up for a new service or app, chances are you’ve been asked to provide your phone number. What seems like a small detail often comes at a cost: your personal number is now linked to yet another company, open to data leaks, spam, and tracking.

The Blitz Wallet Solution

With Blitz Wallet’s messaging integration, that problem is solved. Thanks to sms4sats, you can now send and receive SMS without using your personal phone number.

This feature allows users to access disposable, sats-powered SMS. A simple, private way to communicate or register for services on the go.

How It Works

  • Open Blitz Wallet
  • Tap on Store tab at the bottom of the screen
  • Click theSMS card
  • Choose either send or receive
  • Pay instantly with Bitcoin
  • Start browsing privately, right away.

Demo

See it in action in our demo video: Watch here.

Why it matters

  • Protect your identity: Keep your personal number safe from data breaches and unwanted marketing.
  • Safer sign-ups: Register for platforms, trials, or services without exposing yourself.
  • Stress-free sharing: Use temporary numbers whenever you don’t trust a site or service.
  • Lightning-powered privacy: Every SMS is paid instantly with Bitcoin, making it simple and secure.

Looking Ahead
