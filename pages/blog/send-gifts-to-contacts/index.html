Yesterday we kicked off our 14-day feature series with instant gift card purchases. Today, we’re taking it one step further with a feature that has never existed in a Bitcoin wallet before: the ability to send gift cards directly to your friends.

The Gap Before Blitz Wallet

Until now, no Bitcoin wallet allowed you to share gift cards natively. If you wanted to send someone a gift card, you had to buy a code, take a screenshot, or send an email, none of which felt integrated, seamless, or secure. It was clunky, impersonal, and definitely not designed for peer-to-peer sharing.

The Blitz Wallet Breakthrough

Blitz Wallet changes this completely. With just one tap, you can send gift cards directly to your contacts, delivered instantly wallet-to-phone. No codes. No emails. No screenshots. Just direct delivery that feels as natural as sending a text message.

Whether it’s groceries, online shopping, entertainment, or sending a last-minute gift, you can turn your Bitcoin into practical value in just a few taps.

Why It Matters

This feature is more than just a convenience, it’s a breakthrough in how Bitcoin wallets can be used in everyday life.

  • For non-Bitcoiners: You can now share real value with friends and family who don’t yet use Bitcoin. They don’t need to hold BTC to benefit from Bitcoin-powered apps.
  • For onboarding: It’s a fun, approachable way to introduce people to the Bitcoin ecosystem without asking them to buy or manage Bitcoin themselves.
  • For gifting: Birthdays, holidays, or just helping out, sending value has never been this simple.

How it Workds

  • Open Blitz Wallet
  • Tap on the Contacts tab at the bottom of the screen
  • Click a Contact from you list of added contacts or add a new contact
  • Click the arrow pointing up
  • Click the Send Gift pill above the keypad
  • Choose a Gift Card
  • Choose and amount and add an optional description
  • Send!

That’s it. No extra steps, no middlemen, no confusion.

Expanding Bitcoin’s Reach

By making it this easy to share gift cards, Blitz Wallet extends the power of Bitcoin far beyond just Bitcoiners. It creates a gateway for anyone — even those who’ve never touched Bitcoin before — to benefit from a Bitcoin-native tool.
