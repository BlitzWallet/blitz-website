Welcome back to our 14 Days of Blitz series, where each day we showcase a unique feature that makes Blitz Wallet more than just a Bitcoin wallet. Today’s spotlight is all about privacy — and how Blitz Wallet puts control back in your hands with VPN access powered by Bitcoin.

The Privacy Problem

In today’s world, digital privacy is harder than ever to maintain. Traditional VPN services often lock you into monthly or yearly subscriptions, require accounts, and leave you paying for access you might not use regularly. On top of that, your payment details are tied to your identity — the opposite of private.

The Blitz Wallet Solution

Blitz Wallet integrates directly with LNVPN, giving you instant, pay-as-you-go VPN access. With Lightning payments, you can:

  • Pay by the hour or the quarter — no subscriptions, no recurring charges.
  • Stay anonymous — no accounts, no credit cards, no identity attached.
  • Get privacy on demand — activate secure browsing when you actually need it.

It’s simple: your Bitcoin becomes your gateway to private, censorship-resistant internet access.

How It Works

  • Open Blitz Wallet
  • Tap on Store tab at the bottom of the screen
  • Click theVPN card
  • Choose your duration and VPN location
  • Pay instantly with Bitcoin
  • Start browsing privately, right away.

Why It Matters

This feature is about more than convenience, it’s about empowering you to take back control of your digital footprint. By combining Bitcoin and VPNs, Blitz Wallet makes privacy accessible, affordable, and flexible.

Whether you’re traveling, working remotely, or just want a more secure connection, Blitz Wallet gives you privacy on the go, when you want it, not when a subscription says you can have it.
