Our Vision

At Blitz, we have a simple vision: to become the leading payment solutions platform in Bitcoin, with the ultimate goal of bringing Bitcoin payments to the masses. To achieve this, we are focusing on two key areas: usability and abstraction.

Usabilty

For us, usability means that anyone, from a Bitcoin expert to your grandmother, can easily use the app. We strive to achieve this usability through minimalistic design, extensive user testing, and simplifying technical features.

Abstraction

Users should not need to understand how a product works to use it. With this in mind, we have removed the complexities of the technologies we use to simplify the user experience.

Our Strategy

Purpose

To empower users to easily send and receive payments across the globe.

People

Create a simple, KYC-free, and self-custodial application to enable people to transact freely and securely.

Planet

Remove payment barriers and economic inequalities between regions.

Transparency

Embrace the open-source philosophy in all business processes, including idea generation, code, and future plans.
