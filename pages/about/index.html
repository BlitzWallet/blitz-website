The Challenge

Why Global Payments Are Broken

The Problem

The global payments system is fragmented, expensive, and designed around institutions — not people. Whether you're sending money to family overseas, paying a freelancer in another country, or splitting a bill with someone on the other side of the world, you run into the same walls:

  1. High fees – Traditional wire transfers and remittance services take 3–10% of every transaction, robbing families of billions every year.
  2. Slow settlement – Bank transfers can take days. International wires even longer. In 2026, that's unacceptable.
  3. Access barriers – Billions of people worldwide lack access to reliable banking infrastructure, locking them out of the global economy entirely.
  4. No sovereignty – Traditional systems can freeze accounts, reverse transactions, and restrict access at any time. You don't truly own your money.

Bitcoin offers a way out — a global, open, permissionless monetary network that anyone with a smartphone can access. But until now, using Bitcoin for everyday payments has been too complex for most people: confusing addresses, expiring invoices, channel management, and a user experience that feels nothing like the apps people already love.

Our Solution

Blitz Wallet is a global payments app built on Bitcoin's open rails. We combine the best of modern payments UX with the power and sovereignty of Bitcoin — making it possible for anyone to send money instantly, anywhere in the world, at near-zero cost.

At the core is Spark, a Bitcoin-native Layer 2 that enables instant, self-custodial transactions without the complexity of traditional Lightning — no channel management, no inbound liquidity, no node uptime requirements. On top of that foundation, we've built a suite of features designed to make Bitcoin payments feel as natural as any app you already use:

  • Contact-Based Payments – Send Bitcoin to anyone in your contacts by name, not a cryptic address. Zero fees between Blitz users. No invoices. No QR codes required.
  • Blitz Gifts – Send Bitcoin to anyone — even people who don't have Blitz yet — via a shareable gift link. The perfect on-ramp for friends and family.
  • Payment Pools – Coordinate group payments and collections, powered by Lightning. Split costs, collect contributions, and settle instantly — no Venmo required.
  • USDB Stablecoin Savings – Hold your balance in USDB, a USD-pegged stablecoin, to avoid Bitcoin price volatility while staying on Bitcoin's rails. Earn rewards and track your goals — all self-custodial.
  • Stablecoin Integration – Seamlessly swap between Bitcoin and stablecoins to transact in the currency that makes sense for you, without leaving the app.
  • Human-Readable Addresses – Your Blitz address looks like username@blitzwalletapp.com. Send and receive payments as simply as sending an email.

The result: a payments app that feels familiar, works globally, costs almost nothing — and puts you in full control of your money.
